Why attribution matters

Without an MMP, iOS is a black box

Since App Tracking Transparency, the ad networks can't see most of your iOS conversions on their own — installs slide into "unattributed" and your reported ROAS quietly lies to you. A mobile measurement partner is how you get the truth back.

  • AppsFlyer + SKAdNetwork 4.0, configured correctly, rebuild lost signal with privacy-safe conversion values.
  • Deep links, postbacks, and revenue events tie every dollar back to a campaign, creative, and channel.
  • You make budget calls on real numbers — scale what works, cut what doesn't — instead of gut feel.
